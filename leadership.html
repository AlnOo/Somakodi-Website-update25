Workplace Readiness & Leadership Program

4 Weeks

Develop the soft skills to lead, influence, and succeed in today’s dynamic work environment.

The Workplace Readiness & Leadership Program at Somakodi is a soft skills course designed to help learners build essential professional and leadership capabilities. This program equips you with the tools to lead yourself and others, communicate with impact, embrace entrepreneurial thinking, and negotiate with confidence. Whether you're preparing for your first job or looking to grow in your current role, these skills are crucial for long-term success.

Why You Should Join

The Workplace Readiness & Leadership Program is a 4-week practical course designed to help you develop the soft skills needed to thrive in today’s professional world. Whether you're just entering the workforce or looking to grow in your current role, this program will guide you through essential leadership principles and professional behaviors.

We provide blended learning that integrates state-of-the-art technologies in course delivery, combining online resources, live class sessions, and real-world projects.

This course is ideal for students, job seekers, entry-level professionals, and anyone looking to strengthen their soft skills and leadership potential. No prior experience is needed just a desire to grow and succeed.

You’ll build a strong foundation in leading yourself, communicating with impact, entrepreneurial thinking, and negotiation skills. By the end of the course, you'll be more confident, self-aware, and better prepared to lead, influence, and collaborate in any work environment.

What You will Learn

Unit 1: Leading Self

This module focuses on building self-awareness, personal accountability, and the mindset required for success in both life and work. You’ll learn how to set clear goals, manage your time effectively, build confidence, and stay motivated through challenges. We’ll guide you in developing emotional intelligence, self-discipline, and a growth mindset—core qualities that empower you to take initiative, make better decisions, and lead by example in any environment.

Unit 2: Communicating for Impact

Effective communication is at the heart of leadership and teamwork. In this module, you’ll learn how to express yourself clearly, listen actively, and tailor your message to different audiences and situations. We’ll cover verbal and non-verbal communication, presentation skills, giving and receiving feedback, and managing difficult conversations. Whether you're speaking one-on-one, in a team meeting, or presenting your ideas, this module will help you build confidence and influence through intentional, impactful communication.

Unit 3: Entrepreneurship Essentials

This module introduces you to the entrepreneurial mindset—thinking creatively, solving problems, and taking initiative. You’ll learn how to identify opportunities, validate ideas, and develop practical solutions that create value. Whether you aspire to start your own business or innovate within an organization, you'll gain foundational skills in business planning, pitching, and resource management. The module also covers resilience, adaptability, and decision making key traits for anyone navigating today’s fast-changing world of work.

Unit 4: Negotiation Skills

Negotiation is a vital skill for navigating both professional and personal situations. In this module, you'll learn how to prepare for, lead, and close negotiations with confidence and clarity. We’ll explore key principles such as understanding interests versus positions, creating win-win outcomes, managing conflict, and building rapport. Through role plays and real-world scenarios, you’ll practice communicating your value, handling objections, and reaching agreements that benefit all parties. These skills will empower you to advocate for yourself, collaborate effectively, and make informed decisions in any workplace setting.
