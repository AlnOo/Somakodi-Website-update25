Financial Aid & Scholarships

Making Education Accessible for All

At Somakodi, we believe that financial limitations should never stand in the way of a brighter future.

We are committed to making our programs affordable for learners from all backgrounds. Whether you need full or partial support, Somakodi’s financial aid and scholarship options are here to help you start your journey into tech and leadership without unnecessary financial pressure.

Our commitment lies in delivering quality, market-relevant, and accessible education and through our flexible fee payment options and supportive scholarships. we ensure that life-changing learning opportunities are accessible to all.

Our financial aid is designed to assist learners with or currently facing their financing education a strain, and are unable to raise full fee payments at the start of the course due to family or personal financial situations.

What Support Can You Access?

Unit 1: Need-Based Financial Aid

Somakodi’s Need-Based Financial Aid is designed to support learners who demonstrate financial hardship or limited access to funding. We understand that the cost of education can be a barrier, and we’re committed to ensuring that motivated learners aren’t left behind due to financial challenges. This aid is awarded based on a review of your financial situation, including household income, personal circumstances, and supporting documentation. Whether you’re unemployed, underemployed, or supporting a family, this option helps reduce tuition costs so you can focus fully on your learning journey.

Unit 2: Merit-Based Scholarships

Somakodi’s Merit-Based Scholarships recognize outstanding applicants who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership potential, or a strong passion for technology. These scholarships are awarded to individuals who show exceptional promise, dedication, and a commitment to making a positive impact in their communities or future workplaces. Ideal for high achievers and self-starters, this scholarship helps reduce tuition costs and rewards your drive to succeed in the tech or leadership space. Selection is based on your application, motivation letter, and any supporting accomplishments or recommendations.

Unit 3: Women in Tech Scholarship

The Women in Tech Scholarship is Somakodi’s commitment to closing the gender gap in the technology industry. This scholarship supports women who are passionate about joining and pursuing careers in software engineering and data science within the tech space. It is open to women from all backgrounds—whether you're a student, a career switcher, or returning to the workforce. We believe in empowering women to lead in innovation, and this scholarship helps reduce financial barriers so more women can access quality tech education, mentorship, and career opportunities.

Unit 4: PWD & Underserved Communities Grant

At Somakodi, we are committed to creating an inclusive learning environment where everyone has the opportunity to thrive. The PWD (Persons with Disabilities) Grant is specifically designed to support learners living with disabilities who are eager to pursue a career in technology or leadership. This grant helps remove financial barriers and provides additional support to ensure accessibility and success throughout the program. We value diversity and believe that every learner, regardless of physical or learning challenges, deserves the chance to gain skills, grow in confidence, and contribute meaningfully to the tech ecosystem.

Don’t let cost hold you back. Apply for financial support and begin your learning journey today.

Specific scholarships may have their own eligibility criteria, such as limited spaces, gender, Disabilities, or program of study.
