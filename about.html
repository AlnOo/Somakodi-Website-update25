About Us

Based in Nairobi, Somakodi is a dynamic software programming school dedicated to nurturing the next generation of tech professionals. We began our journey with a flagship Software Engineering program and have since grown to offer a diverse range of courses designed to meet the demands of the ever-evolving tech industry. Driven by a passion for excellence, our curriculum has expanded to include cutting-edge programs in Data Science, Cybersecurity, and most recently, Mobile App Development. Each course is crafted to deliver practical, hands-on learning experiences that prepare students to solve real-world problems with confidence. At Somakodi, we believe in learning by doing. Our students engage in immersive projects, collaborative workshops, and personalized mentorship that spark innovation and bring ideas to life. Join Somakodi Africa and begin a transformative journey toward a fulfilling career in technology. Together, we’ll shape a future built on creativity, skill, and opportunity.
